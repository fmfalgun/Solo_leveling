## ðŸ”¥ Top 10 Most Useful Commands

1. **Monitor CPU:** `watch -n1 'awk "{print $14+$15}" /proc/$PID/stat'`
2. **Monitor memory:** `watch -n1 'awk "{printf \"%.1f MB\n\", $2*4/1024}" /proc/$PID/statm'`
3. **Find logs:** `readlink /proc/$PID/fd/1 /proc/$PID/fd/2`
4. **Check state:** `awk '{print "State: " $3}' /proc/$PID/stat`
5. **Check if zombie:** `ps aux | grep $PID | grep '<defunct>'`
6. **Get CPU time:** `awk '{printf "%.1f sec\n", ($14+$15)/100}' /proc/$PID/stat`
7. **Memory leak test:** Loop statm over 60 seconds, check for growth
8. **Find PID:** `systemctl show service -p MainPID | cut -d= -f2`
9. **List threads:** `ls /proc/$PID/task/ | wc -l`
10. **Check blocked:** `cat /proc/$PID/wchan`
